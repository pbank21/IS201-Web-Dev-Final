About Me

For the last 13 years I've had the oppertunity to be able to participate in a wonderful sport called ballroom dancing. I've done everything from team competition all the way to individual competition. In team dancing I danced for the BYU youth ballroom team which is one of the best companies in the nation to dance for, with this team we got multiple national titles and it was such a great opertunity to dance for them. In my individual dancing I got to be in multiple national finals, which have been amazing.

Currently I dance for BYU's ballroom dance company and I am on whats called the showcase company. Most people on my team are next in line for the infamous Tour team. One day I hope to be able to dance on that team and be able to tour the world. Below is a video of my high school team that I danced with, and we held the 2021 national title for that style of dance.
